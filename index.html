Hi, I'm a Ph.D. candidate in social sciences (political science) at California Institute of Technology. Previously, I obtained my MSc in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics with Distinction from London School of Economics and Political Science in July 2015. You can find my curriculum vitae here.

My research interests are in American Politics and Political Methodology, focusing on media, elections and election administration, and political behavior and public opinion.

To study substantive questions in these areas, I develop new quantitative methods and improve upon existing methods, including text analysis tools, survey methods, and causal inference. My work involves designing and implementing large-scale online surveys, as well as analyzing large datasets such as corpora of news articles and voter registration data from states and counties.
