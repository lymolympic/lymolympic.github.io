Hi, I'm a Postdoctoral Scholar at the Institute of Politics and the LeRoy Collins Institute at Florida State University. Previously, I obtained my PhD in Social Sciences (Political Science) from California Institute of Technology and my MSc in Econometrics and Mathematical Economics with Distinction from London School of Economics and Political Science.

I work on Political Methodology, and substantively on media, elections and election administration, and political behavior and public opinion.

To study substantive questions in these areas, I develop new quantitative methods and improve upon existing methods, including text analysis tools, survey methods, and causal inference. My work involves designing and implementing large-scale online surveys, as well as analyzing large datasets such as corpora of news articles and voter registration data from states and counties.
